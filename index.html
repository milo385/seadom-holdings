About

Milo Avidane

With over 35 years of hands-on experience in the HVAC and mechanical construction industry, Milo Avidane brings a rare combination of field knowledge, leadership, and strategic insight. As the Owner and Operator of GSI Systems Inc., he has worked alongside some of the most respected mechanical contractors in New York City, delivering high-performance solutions across complex commercial, industrial, and high-rise projects.

Milo specializes in guiding projects from concept through completion — ensuring every phase is executed with precision, efficiency, and cost awareness. His deep understanding of how HVAC systems are designed, priced, and installed allows him to identify unnecessary markups, streamline procurement, and uncover real opportunities for savings.

Known for his integrity, technical expertise, and no-nonsense approach, Milo partners with general contractors, property owners, and managers to bring clarity to HVAC bids — helping them make informed decisions and protect their bottom line before construction begins.

With decades of established relationships across New York's top mechanical subcontractors, suppliers, and equipment vendors, Milo leverages an extensive network to ensure clients receive the most competitive pricing and reliable execution on every project — an advantage no spreadsheet or software can replicate.
